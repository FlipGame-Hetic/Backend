# Characters

### Numbers

- **4 characters** planned in total
- V1 differentiation: **colour changes only** (previously distinct 3D models)

### Ability structure

Each character has:

- **1 Bonus**: Usable in both PvE and PvP modes, helps to score points or defend oneself
- **1 Penalty**: Inflicts a handicap on the opponent (PvP only; disappears in PvE)

### Inspirations and archetypes

- **RoboCop**: Light/protection theme, defensive
- **Judge Dredd**: “I am the law” – relentless justice
- **Cyberpunk Hacker**: Multipliers, portals, terrain manipulation
- **Aggressive Cyborg**: Aggressive theme, offensive (potentially slightly stronger)

**Note:** The final characters are yet to be confirmed, but the bonus/penalty structure has been approved.
